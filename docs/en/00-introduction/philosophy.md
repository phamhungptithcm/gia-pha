# Philosophy

## Product Principles

### Respect tradition, design for modern life
BeFam keeps cultural context intact while fitting modern usage patterns: short
sessions, mobile-first interactions, and distributed families.

### Clarity over complexity
Users should understand who they are in the clan, what they can do, and what
happens next without technical language.

### Trust must be visible
Critical flows (membership, billing, funds, scholarship) should expose status
clearly and keep auditable history.

### Security by default
Data access follows clan scope and role scope. Payment activation follows
confirmed server-side processing, not optimistic assumptions.

### Inclusive for all generations
The product must stay practical for both elder users and younger members.
Technology should reduce friction, not add ceremony.
