# Problem

Modern family clans face recurring operational gaps:

- genealogy records are fragmented and hard to maintain
- lineage context is unclear for younger or remote members
- memorial days and clan events are easily missed
- fund and scholarship activity lacks transparent tracking
- membership onboarding is inconsistent and difficult to verify

The challenge is not just data storage. The real challenge is continuity:
keeping family identity, participation, and trust active across distance,
multiple schedules, and multiple generations.
