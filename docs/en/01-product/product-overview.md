# Product Overview

_Last reviewed: March 17, 2026_

BeFam is a mobile-first genealogy and clan operations platform designed for
Vietnamese family clans that need both cultural continuity and practical daily
coordination.

## Why BeFam Exists

Families are now distributed across cities and countries. People still care
about heritage and clan participation, but modern schedules and distance make
coordination much harder.

BeFam addresses this gap by combining:

- lineage clarity (who belongs where, and how members are related)
- operational continuity (events, funds, scholarship workflows)
- secure access (right member, right clan, right permissions)

## Who BeFam Serves

- clan owners and clan administrators
- branch leaders and branch administrators
- members across generations, including those living far from home
- families onboarding new members through review workflows

## Core Capabilities

### Genealogy and relationship clarity
- structured genealogy by clan and branch scope
- relationship commands for parent-child and spouse links
- interactive genealogy reading with contextual member details

### Clan operations in one workspace
- dual calendar events (solar and lunar)
- funds module with transactions and running balance
- scholarship program and submission/review baseline
- profile and settings workspace baseline

### Secure membership and governance
- phone OTP and child-access sign-in
- member-claim session linking
- discovery and join-request workflow

### Billing and subscription lifecycle
- annual tiered plans by member count
- VNPay-first checkout flow in mobile UX
- explicit payment states: success, pending settlement, failed/canceled
- entitlement activation only after confirmed successful payment processing
